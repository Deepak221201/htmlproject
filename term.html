Term Paper typically a course where students are required to research and write a significant academic paper on a specific topic within their field of study. These courses often focus on developing research skills, critical thinking, and effective academic writing. The course may include components such as selecting a research topic, conducting literature reviews, gathering data, analyzing findings, and presenting conclusions.
